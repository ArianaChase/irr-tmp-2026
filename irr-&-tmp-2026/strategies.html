Citation
Credibility InflateSAIL was built by the University of Surrey, who were pioneers in later years of space debris removal with their mission RemoveDEBRIS.
Summary This paper describes the promising effects of inflatable drag sails as demonstrated through their cubesat mission in 2017. The authors argue that drag sails are a cheap and highly effective way of quickly de-orbiting satellites, but propose it as a technology that is embedded onto new satellites rather than as a removal method (no docking technique was discussed).
Relevance While this supports the effectiveness of inflatable drag sails (agreeing with another source), it's a different type of sail (plastic vs foam) and doesn't address docking maneuvers at all. This is frowned upon by other sources, who prioritize first contact as a means of "most effective method."
